# tareknabihnyu.github.io
Project Name: You can do it

Project Description: The project is a website that promotes pictures and a story of my fitness transformation from being skinny to become muscular. The overall concept and theme of the website is to inspire people who are struggling with their body image and fitness goals. The website showcases my journey from being a skinny person to a muscular one, and the steps I took to achieve my fitness goals. The website aims to create a positive and motivational experience for its visitors.

Process:

To implement the concept, I used the w3.schools website to learn about creating css classes and templates to better understand how to manipulate the webpage. The following steps were taken to create the website:

Designing the website: I used Adobe Photoshop to create a layout design for the website, including the color scheme, fonts, and images.

Developing the website: I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to develop the website. The website has one main page that you can transverse through by the navigation bar at the top, including a part for my story and a picture of me, then a calculator to make help people know how much protein and calories they need a day. Then pictures of me and my friends showing the transformation of us. Then a video about my transformation in detail. Then left a part in which people can contact me if needed.

Adding pictures and content: I selected the pictures that best represented my transformation as well as my friends and added them to the website. I also wrote a detailed story about my journey, which was added to the website.

Testing the website: I tested the website on various devices to ensure that it was responsive and functional.

Reflection/Evaluation:

The project was successful in achieving its goal of inspiring people to achieve their fitness goals. The website received positive feedback from its visitors –my friends– and they acknowledged its ability to help motivate others to pursue their fitness goals. The experience of creating the website was a very challenging yet rewarding process, and I learned a lot about web development and design. Overall, I am satisfied with the outcome of the project and the impact it can have on others.
